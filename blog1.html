teletype.online

16.10.6

Teletype.online is a website designed to promote a product that is a messenger used to connect businesses and managers. The website opens up with an animation that brings the reader in and displays the product.

This site is very minimal but has very good usage of color, animation, and hierarchy. The customization of the color for the site gives personality to the product as well as gives the reader the opportunity to pick a color. The animated image that displays the product being presented shows the emotions that product can present while printing. The visual hierarchy draws the eyes to the product when the site is opened up and brings the attention to the center of the page.

The site is very simple but it lacks details the user might need to understand the product. The navigation is confined to scrolling down and up. There is also no text explaining the sections that the site contains. Agency info for the creators is on the bottom right but it is not very visible.
